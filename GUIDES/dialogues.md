# Bata, Takbo! Dialogue Guide

This document contains a comprehensive list of all dialogues, pop-ups, and tutorial texts used across the game. It is organized by the screen or feature where they appear.

## 1. Main Menu & Welcome Sequence
These dialogues appear when the user logs in and presses the play button for the first time, or when they interact with specific main menu features.

*   **Gesture Setup Prompt (Step 1 / 2):** "Welcome to Bata, Takbo! This game is controlled with your hand gestures through your webcam. Before you play, we need to set up your gestures so the game knows how you move."
*   **Gesture Setup Prompt (Step 2 / 2):** "You'll train 5 gestures: UP, DOWN, LEFT, RIGHT, and REST. It only takes a minute. Ready?"
*   **Tutorial Choice:** "Your hand gestures are already set up! Would you like to continue with the gameplay tutorial, or skip it and go straight to the action?"
*   **Logout Confirmation:** "Are you sure you want to log out?" (Subtext: "You will need to log back in to play.")
*   **Guest Warning:** "You're playing as a GUEST. If you leave now, your progress, gestures, and tutorial completion will NOT be saved."

---

## 2. Gesture Training Setup
These texts guide the user through setting up their webcam and calibrating their hand gestures for the game's controls.

*   **Intro:** "Welcome to Bata, Takbo! Before we begin, let's set up your hand gestures. You'll use your webcam to teach the game how you move!"
*   **Camera Check:** "This is your camera view. Make sure your hand is clearly visible inside the frame!"
*   **UI Explanation:** "These are the gesture buttons. You will select a direction before you start recording."
*   **Training UP:** "Let's train the UP gesture. Make a clear upward hand signal, then press and hold the Record button until the bar fills."
*   **Training DOWN:** "Great! Now do the same for DOWN. Make a downward signal and hold record."
*   **Training LEFT:** "Now point LEFT."
*   **Training RIGHT:** "And point RIGHT."
*   **Training REST:** "One more — make your REST pose. This is what your hand looks like when you are NOT moving (e.g., an open palm or fist)."
*   **Testing:** "Perfect! Let's test it. Click 'Test My Gestures', move your hand, and see if the arrows highlight correctly."
*   **Completion (First Time):** "You're all set! Your gesture model has been saved. Let's jump into the game!"
*   **Completion (Retraining):** "You're all set! Your gesture model has been saved. You can come back anytime to retrain."

---

## 3. Gameplay Tutorial Sequence
These dialogues are shown during the playable gameplay tutorial to teach the user how to move, dodge, and attack.

*   **Welcome to Grid:** "Welcome to the battlefield! This is a 5×5 grid. You stand on it and must dodge the boss attacks."
*   **Move Up:** "Use your hand gestures to move. Try going ↑ UP!"
*   **Move Down:** "Nice! Now try ↓ DOWN."
*   **Move Left:** "Good! Try ← LEFT."
*   **Move Right:** "Almost there — try → RIGHT!"
*   **Free Movement:** "Great! Move freely for a bit — get comfortable with the controls."
*   **Attack Warning:** "The boss will attack you! Watch the RED tiles — those show where danger is coming."
*   **Dodge Instruction:** "DODGE! Move away before the attack hits!"
*   **Damage Explanation:** "If you stand on a tile when it explodes — you take damage. Watch the bottom for your HP!"
*   **Counter-Attack Intro:** "Now it's YOUR turn. See the glowing golden tile? Step on it to damage the boss!"
*   **Practice Round:** "Let's put it all together! Dodge the boss, then step on the golden tile when it appears."
*   **Tutorial Complete:** "You're ready for the real action! Good luck, warrior!"
